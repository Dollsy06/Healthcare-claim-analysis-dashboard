**Project overveiw**

The Healthcare Claim Analysis Dashboard is a data analytics project built using Power BI to analyze and visualize healthcare insurance claim data.

The goal of this project is to identify patterns in claim approvals, rejection rates, claim amounts, hospital performance, and patient trends to support better decision-making in the healthcare insurance domain.

**Objectives**

Analyze total claim volume and claim amount

Identify approval and rejection trends

Monitor claim status distribution

Track claim trends over time


**Tools & Technologies Used**

Power BI

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

** Key Dashboard Features**
1️⃣ **Claim Overview**

Total Claims

Total Claim Amount

Approved Claims

Approval Rate %

2️⃣** Claim Status Analysis**

Distribution of Approved, Rejected & Pending claims

Visualized using Pie / Donut charts

3️⃣ **Claim Amount Analysis**

Claim Amount by Hospital

Claim Amount by Treatment Type

Claim Amount by Insurance Provider

4️⃣** Time-Based Analysis**

Monthly Claim Trend

Yearly Comparison

Claims Growth Pattern

5️⃣** Hospital & Patient Insights**

Top Hospitals by Claim Value

Top Patients by Claim Count
